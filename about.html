About Us

Welcome to EscapeVerse, an interactive learning platform that turns studying into an adventure!

EscapeVerse is an innovative web app designed to make learning fun and engaging. It combines AI-powered quizzes, interactive puzzles, and an escape-room-style gameplay to help users master technical subjects. Players can select a subject, take an AI-generated quiz to assess their level, and then solve progressively harder challenges. A leaderboard system keeps the competition alive, motivating users to improve their skills.
